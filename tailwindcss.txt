mx-auto: To set the content within the container(in this case it gives gap to logo and text from the left or right side respectively)
flex items-center: To get all the container items along a line or along the length of the container and not top to bottom
justify-between: Arranges all the items all along the length of the container such that they are equally spaced out
ml-6: Margin left applies
px and py: Horizontal and vertical padding i.e gives specified space from top and bottom for py and from left and right for px
For the class section, vertical padding was py-16 originally but changed to py-8 as to remove the scroll option
